MY LIFE.

My name is Nana Sarpong Akosah. I am a twenty seven-year-old Ghanaian man who hails from Anomabo in the Central Region, but lives at Pokuase in the Greater Accra Region. I have a chocolate skin colour, weigh 50kg and 5'4 ft tall.

A selfie of Nana.

I am a lover of video games and sports, specifically, athletics and football. I attended Amerol Nursery School for my pre-school education, after which I attended Falsssyyyd Foundation School for my basic education.

I went on to further my education in Presbyterian Boys' Senior High School where I studied General Arts with Economics, Geography, Elective Mathematics and Government as elective subjects. After spending three years in the second cycle education system, I was enrolled in University of Ghana, Legon for my tertiary education where I studied Geography and Sociology (combined).
